# PawProof content context

## Product truth

PawProof is a multi-pet care journal for iOS and web. It organizes pet profiles, vaccines, reminders, medications, vet visits, documents, emergency details, and PDF health summaries. Verify the current repository before describing a feature. Do not claim veterinary integrations, automatic clinic syncing, Android availability, or medical decision support unless those features exist.

## Audience and positioning

Write for busy pet owners, especially multi-pet households, people managing older pets or recurring care, and owners preparing records for vets, boarding, daycare, travel, sitters, or emergencies. Use calm, practical language. Position PawProof as an organization tool, not a veterinary service.

## Priority topic clusters

1. Pet medical and vaccine record organization
2. Medication, refill, appointment, and booster reminders
3. Multi-pet household coordination
4. Boarding, daycare, sitter, travel, and emergency record readiness
5. Vet handoffs, visit history, and document scanning
